Key AI Foundations: The Power of Distinction

Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS): The Strategic Planner

MCTS is our AI's "grandmaster chess player," simulating millions of future scenarios to find optimal long-term strategies, not just immediate best steps. It excels where rules are rigid and LLMs lack planning depth.

1. Selection

Choose the most promising path from current state, balancing exploration & known good options.

2. Expansion

Add new, unexplored actions (child nodes) to the tree from the selected node.

3. Simulation

From new nodes, run quick, randomized "rollouts" to see possible long-term outcomes.

4. Backpropagation

Use simulation results to update statistics of all nodes in the path, teaching the tree what works.
